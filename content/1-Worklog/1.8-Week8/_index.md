### Week 8 Objectives:

* Learn Amazon CloudWatch metrics, alarms, and logs.
* Understand how to monitor AWS resources such as EC2 and RDS.
* Create basic CloudWatch alarms and notifications.
* Explore CloudWatch Logs for troubleshooting and analysis.

### Week 8 Achievements:

* Viewed CloudWatch metrics for key services (EC2, RDS, Lambda…) and learned how to read graphs and time ranges.

* Created basic CloudWatch alarms (for example, CPUUtilization) and configured simple notification actions.

* Explored CloudWatch Logs:
  * Viewed and filtered Lambda and EC2 logs
